/ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

The things people ask us most. Still curious? Call the gym.

What is O2 BodyFit Boot Camp anyway?

O2 BodyFit Bootcamp in Daly City is a fitness program that includes top notch fitness instructions, nutritional guidance and motivational training all designed to get you in the best shape of your life in the shortest, safest possible time regardless of your current fitness level.

What makes O2 BodyFit Boot Camp different from other ones?

Everything is geared toward YOU as an individual. We accommodate members of ALL fitness levels, because we always give modifications for beginners, as well as for advanced members.

Our program is also unique because we are focused on more than just the physical aspect of fitness.

I feel a little nervous about getting started. I have never done anything like this before.

Many new clients felt that way — it’s completely okay. What we find is after you meet us, you will feel exceptionally comfortable with us and our training approach.

What if I can’t attend all the days offered?

Some exercise is better than no exercise. So if you can’t make it four days a week, at least do your best to attend two or three days a week.

What do I need to bring?

Just bring a water bottle, sweat towel, yoga mat, and a positive attitude. We’ll provide all the equipment, motivation, and accountability you’ll need.

How soon will I see results?

Performance improvement can happen in as little as a couple of days. Visual improvement can take from 2-4 weeks depending on your effort level.

Does the boot camp ever end?

Our bootcamp in Daly City is an ongoing program. After all, it’s a lifestyle change. You’re welcome to join for as little as 1 month or stay on board for the long haul like 85% of our members.

What if I have other questions?
